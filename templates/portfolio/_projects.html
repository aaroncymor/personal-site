Pawfect Pair

February 2021

Visit Site

Introduction

SM, a big company known for their malls, partnered with PAWS (Philippine Animal Welfare Society), and Pet Express for a project about adopting stray dogs and cats. People can visit and view a list of dogs and cats they want to adopt. They can also share the pet info through Facebook and Twitter! This app was featured on the news, here and here!

Challenges

Having only 1 year experience with Wordpress, I have not really dived deeper into this technology since it's really overwhelming. With only two weeks to finish this project, and little familiarity with Wordpress templating, this was really challenging.

Approach

I'm so used to creating shortcodes, but this project is going to need more than that. I need to also change the header and footer of the website, since this web app doesn't really have a navigation. It's actually more of a mobile app rather than a full website. I used wordpress templates for this, and this had me studying how it works for days, but was worth the trouble. I used plain CSS for styles, and vanilla Javascript only!

Rising Tide Digital

February 2020

Visit Site

Introduction

The company wanted a new look and feel for their website. It was already built with wordpress. The goal is to successfully interpret all the templates made by our Web Designer into fully functioning website.

Challenges

Having only 8 months of experience in PHP, and Wordpress, I needed to refresh my knowledge about these technologies since the last time I used them was around 2015. Also, I have been working more on backend related stuff lately, than in the frontend so I'm not that confident with my HTML, CSS, and Javascript skills. By allotting time to refresh my knowledge on frontend, PHP, & Wordpress I managed to complete the website on time. I was able to create wordpress shortcodes to generate custom cards. Website responsiveness was not that difficult anymore when I learned how to incorporate flex boxes, and grids. The approach made me became less reliant on Bootstrap, and other CSS frameworks.
